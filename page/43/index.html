The Infinite Scrollbar

A couple of ajax-type web apps have added “infinite scroll” implementations. The infinite scroll being that when you get near the bottom of the results page it dynamically loads more results. There’s no real paging, just loading. The two apps I’ve seen it in now are the new Google Readerwhen viewing a subscription and the new A9when viewing search results. Supposedly Live.com Search has/had this but doesn’t seem to do it now in Firefox....
